  cold-start is dominated by the ~11 GiB public-base download + torch.compile, NOT by
  the dequant-ple step. Measured margin (624.96 s) is in fact larger than #801's plain
  int4head 470 s margin; the difference is environmental (torch.compile cache lives
  under ~/.cache/vllm, outside HF_HOME, so it can be partially warm across runs), not
  the dequant-ple delta. Even a fully-cold torch.compile (~+50 s) keeps the margin
  > 550 s — comfortably fire-safe.
